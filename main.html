The Parthenon is a former temple on the Athenian Acropolis, Greece, dedicated to the goddess Athena. Built in the 5th century BC, it is considered a masterpiece of Greek architecture.
The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is an ivory-white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
